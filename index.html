I've been working in television production for 14 years. After dabbling here and there for awhile with HTML and CSS, I finally took the leap and began to teach myself front end web development. I've been on this journey for about two years now, gaining proficiency with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Typescript along the way. In the process, I've learned and become a fan of Angular and Bootstrap frameworks.

As with many self-taught developers, my sources for learning are varied. I've been a part of The Odin Project since I started and eventually discovered freeCodeCamp. I'm also a student at Bay Valley Tech Code Academy here in Central California. They provide free training and support for new web-developers. I'll be graduating from their program in late Spring 2021.

Thanks for stopping by. Feel free to check out my projects and drop me a line!
